## Challenge Statements

1. Trust, Commerce & Fraud... Build systems that make online transactions safer, reduce scams, detect fraud, and increase trust in digital marketplaces.


2. The Future of Work... Help people navigate an increasingly volatile job market and access better career opportunities.


3. Security, Resilience & Defense... Strengthen national, organizational, and individual resilience against physical and cyber threats.


4. AI-Native Organizations... Help businesses transition from traditional workflows to AI-powered, agent-driven operations. Rapidly. At scale.



These are suggestions, not constraints. The best projects often come from a problem you've personally experienced and deeply understand.

## Judging Criteria

Sup 2026 (Presenting)

Judging criteria

Criterion Weight Question

Proof of Work - Functionality 25% Does it actually work? Did the team ship a real demonstrable build?
Problem fit and & Market Value 25% Is this a real problem with a real user? How commercially viable is this? If building for public good - will this be adopted and impactful at scale?
Design, Craft & Taste 20% Is it purposeful and tasteful? Is the user experience intuitive and well-considered?
Innovation & Creative use of sponsor technology 30% Is the core idea genuinely fresh or a known concept with a new coat of paint? Is a sponsor tool (OpenAI/Codex, Exa, Cursor, Zo) used in a central, inventive way and not just bolted on?
